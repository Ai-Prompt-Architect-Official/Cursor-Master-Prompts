## The Problem: AI Context Collapse

Modern AI coding assistants like **Cursor** and **Claude** are incredibly powerful — but as your project grows, they start losing track of your architecture. This leads to:

- ❌ Generated code that ignores your folder structure
- ❌ Inconsistent patterns across your codebase
- ❌ AI "hallucinating" imports and dependencies
- ❌ Hours spent correcting AI output instead of shipping features

## The Solution: Master Prompts

**AI Prompt Architect** generates comprehensive, best-practice **Master Prompts** — structured architectural blueprints that tell your AI assistant exactly how your project works.

Each Master Prompt includes:

- 📁 **Folder structure mapping** — Your project's exact layout
- 🛠️ **Tech stack context** — Frameworks, libraries, and conventions
- 🔀 **Mermaid.js data flows** — Visual architecture diagrams your AI can parse
- 📏 **Coding standards** — Naming conventions, patterns, and anti-patterns
- 🧩 **Component relationships** — How modules connect and communicate

## How It Works

1. **Input your tech stack** — Select your language, framework, database, and tools
2. **AI generates your Master Prompt** — A comprehensive architectural blueprint
3. **Paste into Cursor / Claude** — Your AI assistant now understands your project perfectly
4. **Ship better code, faster** — Clean, consistent, maintainable code from day one
